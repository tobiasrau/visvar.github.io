See this poster for how collecting and visualizing training data can help musicians learn their instruments more efficiently. Another poster demonstrates visualization-supported collaboration between user and AI for composition.

Future work: Leverage VR/AR, motion capturing, and ML/AI to achieve better and more situated visual support for musicians.
