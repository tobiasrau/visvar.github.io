Quynh Ngo, Ph.D.

Quynh does research in the field of Visualization. His recent research interests include dimensionality reduction Techniques, data transformation, graph layout, applying machine learning to visualization. He is also interested in studying the potential novel hybrid techniques among different fields of visual computing, such as visualization, eye-tracking, and virtual/augmented reality.
